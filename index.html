Fractional Biprime Factorization

This static site presents the Fractional Factorization Method dedicated to biprime numbers (i.e., products of two prime numbers). The method is based on fractional decompositions such as dividing the number by values like 1.25, 1.75, etc., and checking if the quotient is divisible by powers of 2.
